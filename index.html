Setia Pramana

PROFESSOR OF STATISTICS

Born in Jakarta, July 22nd, 1977, he is a Civil Servant - Lecturer in one of the official college within the BPS Statistics Indonesia known as the Polytechnic of Statistics STIS. As a lecturer, he has experiences in teaching and even being active in organizational management, national and international seminars, writing and reviewing scientific papers, community service, and guiding D4/S1/S2/S3 students.
